## 3. Fabric Images and Pattern Storage

### 3.1 Fabric Image Handling
**Important: Your fabric images are treated as temporary, session-based data.**

- **Upload:** When you upload fabric images, they are transmitted securely to our servers
- **Processing:** Images are immediately sent to our AI service (Anthropic Claude) for pattern generation
- **Storage Duration:** Images are stored only for the duration of your active session
- **Deletion:** Images are automatically deleted when:
  - Your session ends
  - You log out
  - Pattern generation is complete
  - The session expires (typically within 24 hours)
- **No Long-Term Storage:** We do not permanently store your fabric images on our servers

### 3.2 Generated Patterns
- **Current:** Patterns are generated and provided to you for immediate download
- **Future Feature:** We may add optional pattern history storage, allowing you to access previously generated patterns
- **If Added:** You will be able to view, download, and delete stored patterns from your account
- **Default:** Pattern storage will be opt-in; we will not store patterns unless you enable this feature

### 3.3 Your Rights Regarding Images
You retain all rights to your fabric images and can:
- Delete uploaded images at any time during your session
- Request immediate deletion of any stored data
- Export all your data (if we add pattern storage)
